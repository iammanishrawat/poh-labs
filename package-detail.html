Package Details

The Complete Blood Count (CBC) is one of the most commonly ordered blood tests that provides important information about the types and numbers of cells in the blood, including red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. This comprehensive test helps healthcare providers evaluate your overall health and detect a wide range of disorders.

A CBC measures several key components and features of your blood, including hemoglobin (the oxygen-carrying protein in red blood cells), hematocrit (the proportion of red blood cells to the fluid component of blood), and the actual number of red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets in your blood sample.

This test is essential for diagnosing conditions such as anemia, infections, blood clotting problems, blood cancers, and immune system disorders. It's also used to monitor existing health conditions and evaluate how treatments are working.
